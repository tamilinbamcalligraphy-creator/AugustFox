About Me

Crafting Beauty Through Letters

Hello, I'm Arjun

I am a lettering and calligraphy artist. A successfull Level 2 seller in Fiverr with over 5 years of experience, based in Bangalore, India, and I work with international clients across the world through online collaboration. Services

What I Offer

Handwritten Logo Design

I create unique handwritten logo designs using my handmade folded pens, a traditional technique that brings an authentic ink-splatter texture to every logo. Each piece is crafted entirely by hand, giving your brand a raw, organic, and timeless character that digital fonts can't replicate. In a world moving back toward authenticity, handwritten logos are becoming increasingly popular for their personal, premium, and memorable appeal. My logos are not just designs—they are artworks with soul, created to make your brand stand out globally.

Custom T-Shirt Design

I design original handwritten logos for brands and businesses, created entirely by me with an artistic, hand-crafted approach. Each design carries a bold, authentic identity that stands apart from mass-produced graphics. If required, I also offer complete T-shirt production, including sourcing quality garments and professionally printing the designs for a refined, long-lasting finish. The result is a premium wearable piece that represents your brand with confidence and originality.

Calligraphy Engraving

I use my calligraphy and lettering skills to create custom engravings on keychains, mugs, and various personalized items. Each piece is thoughtfully crafted, making it perfect for gifts, friends, loved ones, or special occasions—turning everyday objects into meaningful keepsakes.

Handwritten Calligraphy Letters & Frames

I create elegant handwritten calligraphy letters, including poems, thank-you notes, and meaningful quotations. Each piece is thoughtfully crafted by hand and paired with relevant hand-drawn illustrations or framed visuals, making every creation personal and emotionally rich. Perfect for gifts, special occasions, and lasting memories, these handcrafted calligraphy artworks bring warmth, beauty, and a timeless human touch.

Custom Tattoo Designs

I create hand-drawn custom tattoo designs based on the reference images or ideas shared by my clients. Each design is carefully illustrated by hand, transforming personal inputs into meaningful, artistic tattoo-ready artwork. Every tattoo design is unique, thoughtfully detailed, and crafted to reflect your story with authenticity and precision.

Calligraphy & Handwriting Classes

I teach cursive handwriting and calligraphy through Glanys Art Studio, Brookfield, focusing on strong fundamentals, creativity, and confidence. My classes are suitable for beginners and learners of all ages, with guided practice and personalized attention. Designed to help students develop beautiful handwriting while enjoying the art of lettering.
